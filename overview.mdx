## Davia apps are single-page applications

They follow a unique architecture combining static editable content with interactive components.
Each Davia app is a complete, standalone application that combines content, functionality, and interactivity into one unified page.

### All your apps in one workspace

All your pages are aggregated in your **workspace** which serves as your **personal app hub**.
Bear in mind that once you import an app to your workspace, it becomes your personal copy. Any data stored is specific to your usage and remains private to you.

### Create, edit apps and navigate your workspace using the agent

Davia's assistant can create or edit your apps through natual language.
Simply describe what you want, and it will build it for you.
Access in right sidepane with command ⌘K or by clicking on the <ChatIcon /> icon.
